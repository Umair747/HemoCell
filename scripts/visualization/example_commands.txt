#Example commands for converting xmf files to x3d and then rendering the result

!!!! You need pvpython to convert xmf to x3d (pvpython comes with the instillation of Paraview)

#Convert xmf to x3d using Paraview
pvpython convert_xmf_to_xd3_oneCellShear.py

#Render the result
blender -b -P render_oneCellShear.py
